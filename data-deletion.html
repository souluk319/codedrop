If you created a CodeDrop account, use the in-app account withdrawal flow. CodeDrop verifies your password, deletes your leaderboard entries, and deletes your user account.

You may also request deletion by emailing souluk319@gmail.com. Include your CodeDrop nickname and state that you want your account and leaderboard entries deleted.

KUGNUS-BRAIN Threads Data Deletion

KUGNUS-BRAIN is a private local desktop assistant operated by the owner. The owner can delete local Threads snapshots and generated reports from the local workspace at any time. Public Threads data retrieved for review and automation is not sold or redistributed.

Response Time

Deletion requests are handled as soon as practical. If extra identity verification is needed, we may ask for additional confirmation before deletion.
