What This App Does

CodeDrop is the public deployed web app operated by the developer. KUGNUS-BRAIN is the developer's private single-user knowledge assistant workflow. The Threads feature watches a small owner-selected list of public Threads profiles and turns public posts into private local research notes.

Why Threads Profile Discovery Is Needed

The owner can already authenticate with Threads OAuth and read the owner account with threads_basic. The missing capability is retrieving public posts from selected public profiles that are not the authenticated owner account. The official Threads API endpoint GET /profile_posts?username=... requires threads_profile_discovery.

Actions Not Performed

The Threads integration does not publish, delete, like, reply, repost, message, share, sell, or publicly redistribute Threads data. It only summarizes selected public posts into private owner notes and notifications.
